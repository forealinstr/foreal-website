About Us

Dalian Foreal Instrument Co., Ltd is a professional manufacturer and supplier specialized in construction non-destructive testing instruments. We focus on high-quality, reliable test equipment for civil engineering, construction, bridges, highways and real estate projects.

Our main products include concrete rebound hammers, rebar detectors, corrosion detectors, crack thickness gauges, ultrasonic testers and related calibration equipment. All products are produced under strict quality control to ensure accuracy, stability and long service life.

We have our own production lines and experienced R&D team, which allow us to control quality strictly and provide competitive prices for global customers. We are committed to providing safe, durable and cost-effective testing solutions.
